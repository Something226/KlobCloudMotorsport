Felix Andersson

  • Born: November 2002
  • Favourite Rally Location: Monte Carlo
  • Main Rally Car: Lancia Delta HF Intergrale

Felix Andersson is our current top driver in KlobCloud Motorsport. After years of shitposting on tinder and twitter, he finally decided that he wanted to pick up a new hobby. Rally became that hobby and eversince his debut in the Jämsä rally of 2019 he has kept on climbing the leader boards with nothing to stop him.

Axel Lieback

Our secondary driver Axel Lieback, whilst not performing as well on ice as Andersson, is a force to be reckoned with in the gravel and asphalt scene of rallying. Despite suffering from sever reddit overdosing, he still manages to pull through the most dire of situations found in the World Rally Championship.
