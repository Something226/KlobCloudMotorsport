We are KlobCloud Motorsport. A racing team founded in the mid 2020's with drivers filled with a passion for rallying. With our new line-up for the current season, we hope to bring a new level to the competition with our two main drivers, Axel Lieback and Felix Andersson, specializing in both modern and historical rally cars. Everything between the world renowned Lancia Stratos all the way up to the newest of cars like the Ford Fiesta R5 MK2.

Join the official KlobCloud Motorsport discord for more info!

One of our leading drivers, Felix Andersson, and with his current title "The Monaco Maniac" has crushed the current competition found in the mountain tops of the Monte Carlo Rally. With his immense clutch control and timed shifts, we have never seen anyone as fast as him on the ice roads of Monte Carlo.
